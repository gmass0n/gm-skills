Title: [TYPE] Short, outcome-oriented title

Description

**Type**
- Bug | Story | Task

**Context**
- What happens today, who is affected, and the verified cause or limitation.

**Objective**
- The intended user or product outcome.

**Scope**
- Product/system: responsibility and required change.
- Technical repository/component (when known): responsibility and required change.

**Acceptance criteria**
1. Observable expected behavior.
2. Observable expected behavior.

**Definition of Done**
- Contract, behavior, and focused validation completed.

**Out of scope**
- Explicitly excluded follow-up work.

**Suggestions**
- Optional implementation direction, clearly separated from requirements.

**Risks / open questions**
- Unknowns that need a decision before implementation.

**Affected repositories**
- Repository/component (when known): role in the flow.

**Evidence**
- Jira context, interview decision, or `path:line` — short relevance note.
