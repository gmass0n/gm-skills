Title: Short, outcome-oriented title

Description

**Problem / opportunity**
- What happens today, who is affected, and why this matters now.
- For a Bug: current behavior, expected behavior, and verified impact.

**User story** _(Story only)_
- As a <role>, I want <outcome>, so that <benefit>.

**Outcome and value**
- Intended future state and the user, operational, or business value.

**Scope**
- Included user journeys, business rules, and affected product surfaces.
- Use repository/component names only to clarify ownership, never as a solution plan.

**Acceptance criteria**
1. Observable expected behavior.
2. Observable expected behavior.

**Delivery constraints** _(only when confirmed)_
- Non-negotiable compatibility, security, permission, performance, or platform constraints.

**Out of scope**
- Explicitly excluded follow-up work.

**Suggested delivery decomposition** _(optional; do not create subtasks unless requested)_
1. Subtask title — owner boundary, one deliverable, and verification signal.

**Implementation considerations** _(optional)_
- Non-binding technical direction, clearly separated from requirements.

**Risks / open questions**
- Unknowns that need a decision before implementation.

**Affected repositories**
- Repository/component (when known): role in the flow.

**Evidence**
- Jira context, interview decision, or `path:line` — short relevance note.
